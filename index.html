Current Series

Rhapsody Of Realities.

Rhapsody of Realities is a daily devotional publication that offers teachings and reflections on Christian faith and spirituality. It's produced by the LoveWorld Incorporation, also known as Christ Embassy, an evangelical Christian ministry based in Lagos, Nigeria, led by Pastor Chris Oyakhilome. Rhapsody of Realities typically includes daily readings, scriptures, prayers, and teachings aimed at helping Christians deepen their understanding of the Bible and their relationship with God. It covers various topics such as faith, prayer, healing, prosperity, and living a victorious Christian life.
